# Comprehensive-Corporate-Finance-Evaluation-of-Infosys-Ltd.

Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive corporate finance analysis of Infosys Ltd. using Microsoft Excel. The study evaluates the company’s financial performance, capital structure, cost of capital, cash flows, dividend policy, working capital efficiency, and valuation over a multi-year period.

The project integrates financial theory with practical Excel-based analysis and dashboarding to support informed investment and managerial decision-making.

Objectives

Analyze Infosys’ historical financial performance and stability

Evaluate profitability, liquidity, leverage, and efficiency ratios

Estimate beta, cost of equity, and WACC using CAPM

Perform cash flow and Free Cash Flow to Firm (FCFF) analysis

Assess dividend policy and shareholder return strategy

Conduct valuation analysis using discounted cash flow logic

Present insights through an Excel dashboard

Scope of Analysis
Company Overview

Global IT and digital transformation leader

Asset-light, zero-debt business model

Strong and consistent revenue and profit growth

Financial Ratio Analysis

Profitability: ROE, ROCE, ROA, EBIT margins

Liquidity: Current ratio, quick ratio

Leverage: Debt-to-equity, interest coverage

Efficiency: Working capital cycle and cash conversion cycle

Risk & Cost of Capital

Beta estimation using multiple time horizons

Cost of equity calculated via CAPM

WACC estimation (equity-only capital structure)

Cash Flow Analysis

Historical operating, investing, and financing cash flows

FCFF estimation and projections

Interpretation of long-term cash-generating ability

Dividend & Capital Allocation

Dividend trend and payout ratio analysis

Share buybacks and shareholder value creation

Comparison of dividend valuation models

Valuation & Insights

DCF-based valuation logic

Interpretation of NPV, IRR, and value creation

Strategic and financial recommendations

Dashboard Highlights

Revenue and net income trends

Margin analysis and return ratios

Beta and stock movement visualization

FCFF projections and present value logic

Dividend trends and payout analysis

All insights are summarized using a structured Excel dashboard.

Key Insights

Infosys exhibits strong profitability with stable margins

Zero-debt structure results in very low financial risk

Efficient working capital management supports liquidity

Cost of equity and WACC converge due to no leverage

Cash flows are stable and support long-term valuation

Dividend policy reflects disciplined capital allocation
